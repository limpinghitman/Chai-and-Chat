CALLING ALL READING FC SUPPORTERS!

The Jazz Cafe welcomes all reading FC supporters on match days.
You can find us between Gates 7 & 8 right on the stadium's doorstep.
Win or lose, take advantage of our well stocked bar, relaxing surroundings and tasty food. It's a real community vibe, so you can meet up with your fellow supporters before, during and after the game. Come on URZZZ!

Exclusive Reading FC Offers


Reading FC season ticket holders and staff receive a 10% discount on private hire of the venue. Subject to availability. Discount can only be applied once per year.
Reading FC season ticket holders and staff can take advantage of free entry on arrival for themselves plus one guest for any live music evening including events in December.
