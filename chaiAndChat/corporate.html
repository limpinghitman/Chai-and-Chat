Corporate Events at The Jazz Café

Whether it’s a company celebration, networking evening or an end of year party, The Jazz Café has everything you could need for a successful event with delicious food, a well-stocked bar and entertainment from our resident DJ.

The Jazz Café can accommodate up to 200 guests in an informal reception set up or up to 120 for a sit-down meal with dancefloor

Our Corporate Event Package includes:-

  • Private hire of the venue for your agreed timings
  • Lavish hot & cold buffet or 3 course sit down meal
  • Use of our well-stocked bar and bartenders
  • A stage, microphone and projector to present speeches
  • Fantastic entertainment from our resident DJ
  • Easy venue access and complimentary, onsite parking

This package starts from £28.50 + VAT per person based on a minimum number of 80 guests. For a simpler package, prices start from £22.50 + VAT per person.

To view a sample of our menu, please click here. We are more than happy to cater for any dietary requirements that your guests may have, please just let us know upon booking.

Drinks Packages:

  • Glass of bubbles on arrival: £4.90 per person
  • ½ bottle of wine on the table: £9.25 per person
  • Drinks vouchers from £14.90 per person

The adjoining 4* Voco Hotel provides preferential room rates to Jazz Café guests. Please contact the hotel directly for more information.

Please email info@thejazzcafe.co.uk for more information and to check availability. Please note that prices stated are subject to change, please get in touch for an accurate quotation.
