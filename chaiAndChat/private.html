Private Events at The Jazz Café

Whatever you’re celebrating whether it’s a birthday, anniversary or you just want an excuse to party, The Jazz Café is perfect for your event!

The Jazz Café is a spacious, modern venue and able to cater for gatherings from 65 guests to large parties of 240.

Hire Charge:

Hire prices for The Jazz Café start from £450.00 on a weekday, £500.00 on a Friday and £650.00 on a Saturday.

This rate includes:

  • Hiring the venue for the agreed times
  • Use of the bar with bartenders
  • Dressed venue
  • A member of staff on the door to greet your guests
  • Complimentary, onsite car parking

Food and drink are additional charges.

Food

We have a number of different menus available for you to choose from. Each menu features delicious dishes from around the world including vegetarian and vegan options. We also cater for other dietary requirements, please just let us know in advance of your booking.

Please click here for a sample menu.

Entertainment:

You are more than welcome to hire your own Band or DJ for your event, however we do ask that they have their own PAT tested equipment and Public Liability Insurance. Alternatively, we would be more than happy to book a band or DJ for you. Please let us know at least 6 weeks before your event if you would like us to book entertainment on your behalf. Bands will be booked subject to their availability.

Overnight Accommodation:

Why not make a night of it and stay at The Voco Hotel at Select Car Leasing Stadium?

This 4* hotel kindly offer a special room rate for Jazz Café guests starting from just £60.00 per room. Please contact our team for more details. Please note that the room rates are subject to change and decided by Hotel Management.

Wakes:

Here at The Jazz Café, we understand that planning a wake needs to be as stress-free as possible. That’s why we’ve got fantastic packages at fantastic prices leaving you to take care of yourself and your loved ones at this difficult time.

Our package includes:-

  • Venue hire
  • A delicious buffet
  • Use of the bar with bartenders
  • Whiten linen on the tables
  • White vases for your flowers
  • Background music
  • Use of the projector & screen to show pictures
  • Complimentary, onsite parking

Please email info@thejazzcafe.co.uk or call 01189 681 442 for more details, prices and to check availability.

Please note that all prices stated are for guidance purposes only and are subject to change. Please get in touch for an accurate quotation.
