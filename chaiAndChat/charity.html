Charity Events at The Jazz Café

If you’re searching for a contemporary venue for your next charity fundraising event – look no further!

Here at The Jazz Café, we pride ourselves in offering the very best in customer service, food, drink and live entertainment. Holding your charity event with us is easy and affordable, because after all we know how important it is for you to raise as much money as possible for your cause rather than spend it on the venue.

Our Charity Packages:

Package One - 50-80 guests

  • Dressed venue
  • Choose from a delicious buffet or an exquisite 3 course meal
  • Discount on pre-ordered wine
  • Stage, dancefloor & state of the art sound system with microphones
  • Preferential accommodation rates at neighbouring 4* hotel: Voco Hotel Reading
  • Complimentary, onsite parking

Venue hire starts from £175.00 on a Friday or is free on a Thursday.

Package Two – Over 80 guests

  • As per Package One plus
  • Complimentary venue hire
  • Jazz Experience for 2 Raffle Prize
  • 5% of the bar takings will be donated to your charity

    • Menu prices are charged separately and start from just £12.50 per person. Menu prices are subject to number of guests and the type of menu chosen.

    Please click here for a sample menu.

    You are more than welcome to hire your own DJ for your event; however we ask that they have their own PAT tested equipment and Public Liability Insurance. Alternatively, we would be more than happy to book one of our resident DJs or bands. Please let us know at least 6 weeks before your event if you would like us to book any entertainment on your behalf.

    To check availability and receive a quote today, please send us an email to info@thejazzcafe.co.uk or call 01189 681 442.
